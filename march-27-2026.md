### Work Distribution
- **Expectations & Task Delegation:** Discussed "startup mode" expectations where everyone wears many hats. It's important to commit to tasks and ask for help when overwhelmed.

- **Code Reviews:** We hope to uphold a strict code review processes. Mehar and Andy will review initially, hopefully eventually shifting to peer reviews.
- **Tech discussion (CI/CD):** Need to invest heavily in tests and deployments on pull requests.
